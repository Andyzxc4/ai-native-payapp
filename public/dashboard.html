How to scan:

  3. Point your camera at the QR code
  4. The payment form will open automatically

📱 Camera Not Working?

✨ Easy Solution: Use "Upload QR Code Image" instead!

Take a screenshot/photo of the QR code, then upload it.

Or fix camera permissions:

  • Samsung/Android Chrome:
    • Tap address bar → 🔒 icon → Permissions → Camera → Allow
    • Or: Settings → Apps → Chrome → Permissions → Camera → Allow
  • Samsung Internet Browser:
    • Menu (3 lines) → Settings → Sites and downloads → Site permissions → Camera → Allow
  • iPhone Safari: Use Safari (not Chrome) for camera access
  • After allowing: Refresh page and try again
